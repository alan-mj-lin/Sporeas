How to use:

Pick the department, type in the English and the Chinese text, and click 'Add'. Do this for each announcement block for each department. 'Delete' will delete the most recent block of announcements. 'Clear' will erase all announcements. 'Show' will transition the projection to show the announcements. Note that the 'Show' button will also scale the screen and adjust its text size to make it more presentable.
