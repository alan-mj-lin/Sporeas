During Service:

  1. Go to the "Main" tab
  2. Type in the English Title.
  3. Type in the Chinese Title.
  4. Type in the Hymns.
  5. Hit the "Submit" button.
  6. Go to the projection page to see it update live (example: http://127.0.0.1:9000/toronto). Otherwise hit the button.

During service, you can enter a verse:

  1. Next to "Verse:", enter a book (e.g.: Genesis).
  2. Next to "Passage", enter the chapter/verse (e.g.: 1:1).
  3. Hit the button to update the display.

Verse Display:

Off by default. "Verse Display" lets you show the actual words in the Bible verse(s) on the projection screen.

  1. Go to the "Utility" tab
  2. To enable or disable Verse Display, click on the toggle button so that it says "On" or "Off".
  3. Hit the button to update the display.
  4. Go to the projection page to see it update (example: http://127.0.0.1:9000/toronto). Otherwise hit the button.
  5. (While on the projection page:) Hit the spacebar button on your keyboard to show/hide the verse overlay.

Go to Top of Page

Announcements:

Main Announcements

  1. Go to the "Announcements" tab
  2. Enter the English on the left side.
  3. Enter the Chinese on the right side.
  4. Select the Department.
  5. Hit "Clear" to start from an empty list of announcements.
  6. Hit "Add" to move what you entered into a list of announcements in memory (NOTE: this will immediately display announcements on the projection).
  7. If you make a mistake, you can hit "Delete". (Or: you can go to the projection and directly edit the text. Just click and type.)
  8. Go to the projection page to see it update live (example: http://127.0.0.1:9000/toronto). Otherwise go back to the "Main" tab and hit the button.
  9. Make sure to also update the Bible Reading and Cleaning Groups section (see below).

Bible Reading and Cleaning Groups

  1. Next to "Verse:", enter a book (e.g.: Genesis).
  2. Next to "Passage", enter the chapter/verse (e.g.: 1:1).
  3. Next to "Cleaning Group:", enter the group letter (e.g.: A, B, C, D).
  4. Next to "Dish Washing:", enter the names of people scheduled to wash dishes.

Dark mode:

Off by default. "Dark Mode" lets you save on electricity and eyestrain by making the admin interface darker.

  1. Go to the "Utility" tab
  2. To enable or disable Dark Mode, click on the Toggle Dark Mode button.

The app will automatically remember your selection (unless you clear cache). Note: dark mode does NOT affect the projection.
