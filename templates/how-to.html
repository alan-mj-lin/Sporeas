How to Use this Web App

Connect Morning Prayer Hymn Singing Service Verse Display

Connect:

  1. Go to http://127.0.0.1:9000/admin
  2. Type in your location (for example: toronto).
  3. Hit the "Connect" button.
  4. Hit the button (the page will change to show a new form).

The form page should now look like this:

If you hit the button, a new tab will open to show the current projection display (example: http://127.0.0.1:9000/toronto).

During Morning Prayer:

  1. Type in the hymn numbers.
  2. Hit the "Morning Prayer" button.
  3. Go to the projection page to see it update live (example: http://127.0.0.1:9000/toronto). Otherwise hit the button.

The projection page should look something like this:

During Hymn Singing:

  1. Type in the hymn numbers.
  2. Hit the "Hymns Singing" button.
  3. Go to the projection page to see it update live (example: http://127.0.0.1:9000/toronto). Otherwise hit the button.

The projection page should look something like this:

Go to Top of Page

During Service:

  1. Type in the English Title.
  2. Type in the Chinese Title.
  3. Type in the Hymns.
  4. Go to the projection page to see it update live (example: http://127.0.0.1:9000/toronto). Otherwise hit the button.

During service, you can enter a verse:

  1. In the 1st box, type/select a book (e.g.: Genesis).
  2. In the 2nd box, type the chapter/verse (e.g.: 1:1).
  3. Hit the button to update the display.

Go to Top of Page

Verse Display:

Off by default. "Verse display" is when you click on the projection screen to show the actual words in the verse(s).

  1. To enable or disable it, click on the toggle button so that it says "On" or "Off".
  2. Hit the button to update the display.
  3. Go to the projection page to see it update (example: http://127.0.0.1:9000/toronto). Otherwise hit the button.
  4. Click anywhere on the projection page to show/hide the verse overlay.
